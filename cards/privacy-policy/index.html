1. Introduction

Welcome to CardSensei ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"). CardSensei is a card game application that lets you play classic card games against AI opponents or with other players via local and online multiplayer.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to all versions of CardSensei, including the iOS app, Android app, and web application.

The short version: CardSensei is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data, we don't serve ads, and we don't sell your information to anyone.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your chosen display name
  • Game history and statistics
  • Paused or in-progress game states
  • App preferences (theme, tips toggle, bid settings)

2.2 Online Multiplayer Data

When you use online multiplayer, gameplay data (such as your display name, game state, and player actions) is transmitted to enable real-time play. This data is encrypted in transit.

2.3 Anonymous Training Data

To improve our AI opponents, we collect fully anonymized gameplay statistics such as game outcomes and scoring patterns. This data contains no player names, identifiers, device information, or any data that could identify you.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes only:

DataPurpose
Local preferences & game historyTo provide a personalized game experience and allow you to resume games
Online multiplayer dataTo synchronize real-time gameplay between players
Anonymous training dataTo improve the quality and difficulty of AI opponents

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of CardSensei.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement commercially reasonable measures to protect your data, including industry-standard encryption for all network communication and secure on-device storage. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

CardSensei uses the following third-party service:

Google Firebase Realtime Database

We use Firebase, operated by Google LLC, to power online multiplayer functionality and store anonymous training data. Firebase may process data on servers in the United States or other countries. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy and the Firebase Terms of Service.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, crash reporting tools, or social media integrations.

6. User Accounts

CardSensei currently does not require user accounts or authentication. We may introduce optional accounts in a future update. If accounts are introduced, we will collect only the minimum information necessary, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and you will have the ability to delete your account and associated data at any time.

7. Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you uninstall CardSensei or manually clear app data.
  • Multiplayer data is retained temporarily to support game resumption and is automatically deleted after a period of inactivity.
  • Anonymous training data is retained indefinitely to improve AI quality. Because this data contains no personal information, it cannot be linked back to any individual.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You can delete local data at any time by uninstalling CardSensei or clearing the app's data in your device settings. You can contact us at any time to ask what data, if any, we hold about you.

If you reside in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the GDPR including access, correction, deletion, and portability of personal data. If you reside in California, you have rights under the CCPA including the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information. Because CardSensei currently collects no personally identifiable information, these rights are largely satisfied by default.

9. Children's Privacy

CardSensei is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, please contact us.

10. International Users

CardSensei is available worldwide. If you use online features, your game data may be processed on servers in the United States or other countries. By using these features, you acknowledge that data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own.

11. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time, for any reason, at our sole discretion. When we make changes:

  • The "Effective Date" at the top of this page will be updated.
  • For material changes (such as new data collection or account features), we will provide notice through the App or our website.
  • Your continued use of CardSensei after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
